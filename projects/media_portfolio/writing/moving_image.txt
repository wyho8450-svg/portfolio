This project explores the idea of sketches to reality. As a designer and a fabricator, I bring stuff that is in my imagination to life. Giving my thoughts a physical life. The video moves back and forth between fire and working by hand through drafting, welding, blacksmithing, and leather work. Fire became the center of the metaphor because it represents the birthplace of dreams and the forge of life. The crackling of the fire stays constant through the whole video and ties the acts of making together. What this project made clear to me is how much of my identity is rooted in making. Whether it is drawing, fabricating, or designing, the process is where ideas are tested and shaped. The final result matters, but the process is what gives it meaning.
