I chose Snapchat for this story because it felt like the easiest and most natural place for a joke like this to live. Snapchat is quick and easy to use and people consume it fast, which fit the pacing of the story well. Since the whole point was to make a light joke about AI, it made sense to use a platform that does not take itself too seriously. The story format also helped build up to the punchline one frame at a time. I think posting it somewhere else would have made it feel more formal than it needed to be. Snapchat kept it casual which helped the joke land better.

A pen is my most used tool so I use a fountain pen. This is the tool I used to draw this image. As someone who sketches often, pen feels the most natural to me. There is no erasing or second guessing making this media not only show result but the process too. I think that creates a more honest drawing because it shows process and imperfection at the same time. For this project it allowed me to work quickly and get the idea down without overthinking it.
