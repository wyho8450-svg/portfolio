I took this course as I thought it had much overlap with my true major which is architecture. This class expanded my view of what media actually is and where it is applied and leveraged in my life. It made me pay attention to how media shapes the way we  interact with the world around us.

One of the biggest things I took away from this course was how layered media really is. As someone studying architecture, that idea resonates with me because design works the same way. Architecture can be art, it can be powerful, it can be informative. This course helped me connect those ideas and understand that media, like design, has the ability to influence behavior, emotion, and experience. It made me more aware of the things I create and consume, and more intentional in how I approach both.
