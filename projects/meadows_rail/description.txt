Project Title: Meadows Rail
Role: Designer/Foreman/Fabricator
Date: Summer 2025


The Meadows Hand Rail was one of my first design projects to move from concept to built form. Spanning several years, the project allowed me to step into multiple roles—from early ideation and detailing to fabrication oversight and on‑site coordination. The design transforms a visually heavy material into something unexpectedly light and refined, emphasizing proportion, joinery, and craft. Through this process, I gained meaningful experience in leveraging my technical skills, learning as a mentee, guiding others as a mentor, and carrying an idea all the way from an initial sketch to a realized, physical piece.
