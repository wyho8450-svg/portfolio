Project Title: Grove Hooks
Role: Designer/Mentor/Fabricator
Date: Fall 2025




This installation attempts to take a heavy material and make it look light and organic. It serves as a coat rack in the entrance to this house. The sketches provide a glimpse into the iterative process that takes us from idea to finished product. My role in the project was to design, coordinate, and then mentor members of my family to weld and install the project onsite.
