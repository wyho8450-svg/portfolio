Project Title: Freefall
Role: Designer
Date: Spring 2026


Where gravity becomes levity.
Located behind Point Lookout in Crested Butte, Colorado, Freefall is designed as an earned, contemplative destination reached after a four-mile hike. Two large openings are veiled by continuous sheets of falling water, filtering light and producing a steady acoustic backdrop that intensifies the meditative quality of the space. The southeast orientation captures soft morning light while allowing the atmosphere to evolve throughout the day, reinforcing the project’s focus on stillness, gravity, and release.
