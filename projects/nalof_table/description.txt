Project Title: NALOF Table
Role: Designer/Fabricator
Date: Fall 2025
The NALOF Table is one of my favorite pieces of furniture I’ve designed. Its name—Need‑A‑Lot‑Of‑Friends—reflects both its scale and its spirit. I carried this project from initial concept through final fabrication, allowing the room to guide the selection of the wood slab and letting the character of that slab, in turn, shape the leg geometry. The result is a piece where material, proportion, and context are in constant dialogue. This project strengthened my ability to listen to a space, respond to the inherent qualities of a material, and steward a design from idea to finished object.
